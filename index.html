We Are On A Mission
To Help The Helpless

Northern Intergrated Development Organizations (NIDO) support some of the most vulnerable and poor households in Northern Kenya in Accelerating sustainable private-sector led growth for job creation and income generating activities.

MISSION

NIDO is a non profit organization that works To empower and promote active participation of the vulnerable and disadvantaged members of the society towards self reliance and sustainable development through capacity strengthening, education and quality Health.

VISSION

Northern Intergreted Development Organization(NIDO) exists to provide quality Education for all,Holistic quality Health for underserved population , a better socio-economically empowered society for the benefit of all people in need

CORE VALUES

❖ INTEGRITY: We are committed to honesty, accountability, and transparency in all our endeavors.
❖ INCLUSIVENESS: We will work with others to help the world’s needy, regardless of race, gender, age, creed or nationality, color, political affiliation, gender or belief.
❖PROFESSIONALISM, TEAMWORK, AND EXCELLENCE: NIDO values effective teamwork and collaboration when delivering professional services. We strive to attain the highest standards in all that we do, and we are committed to participatory decision making.

OUR PRINCIPLES

❖Empowerment and Strengthening of capacity of target groups for self reliance.
❖Promoting social and economic development.
❖Promoting linkages and networking with Government and other development agencies to meet the organization’s objectives.
❖Promote equality and equity.

What We Do?

We are dedicating to alleviating human suffering regardless of race,color,religion or cultural background.

Health

Improvement of maternal and child health in Kenya’s marginalized population through health system strengthening initiatives at community and primary care levels. Improve health outcome by strengthening social determinant of health for Orphan and vulnerable Children (OVC) and other disadvantaged groups through education literacy, Nutrition ,water and sanitation.

EDUCATION

NIDO is a non profit organizations focused on closing gaps in education for students from low-income families. Our efforts work to; *Increase equity in education at all levels; *Increase college access and completion, *Engage diverse communities dedicated to education equity, *To promote Essential element for achieving gender equality and promoting women's rights such as Sexual Reproductive Health Right .

COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT

To promote Essential element for achieving gender equality and promoting women's rights such as Sexual Reproductive Health Right ( SRHR ),Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) ,Gender Based Violence (GBV ) and Early Child Marriage ❖ Implements community-level social and behavior change (SBC) approaches that boost uptake of facility-level MNCH health services and improve household- and individual-level behaviors
